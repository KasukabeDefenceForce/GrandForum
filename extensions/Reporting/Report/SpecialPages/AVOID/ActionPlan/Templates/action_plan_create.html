Goals to Action

Think about one thing you can realistically expect to accomplish within the next week related to your goal.

Your action plan should:
  • Be something you want to do
  • Help you solve a problem or reach a goal
  • Be actionable (for example not to lose weight, but rather to drink water instead of juice)
  • Answer the questions What? How much? When? And How often?

Here are a few examples of action plans:
  • Walk after dinner 4 days this week for 20 minutes at a moderate pace.
  • Include protein in at least 2 of 3 meals every day this week.
  • Call and catch up with 2 different friends for at least 20 minutes, 2 days this week.
